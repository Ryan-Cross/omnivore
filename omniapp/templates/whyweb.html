The web via smart phone

When people go online it’s most often on their cell phone. The share of digital traffic coming via mobile continues to increase. People like to use their phones.

Web is best!

So how will you get the word out?

First you should put the information where they’re looking, on the web. A web site is a cheap and effective way to reach the broadest audience.

Second be prepared for visitors on mobile. You have to be sure that whatever device your audience is using to view your site is responsive . A responsive web design will adapt to the viewing port of the users device. It will look good on a smart phone, an ipad or a monitor.
